BUXORO KIBER QALQON — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-28 · App package: uz.buxoro.kiber_qalqon · Contact: murodovichmehriddin@gmail.com

BUXORO KIBER QALQON ("the App") is an on-device cybersecurity tool. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and what is sent off your device.

1. Core principle — on-device processing

Security analysis (SMS classification, RCS/notification fraud detection, app/permission audit, file checks, QR/URL analysis) is performed locally on your device. The content of your SMS, messages, notifications, photos, files, and contacts is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Data we collect

DataPurposeStored where
Anonymous device ID (random SHA-256 hash — not your phone IMEI/number)Aggregate threat statistics and active-user countsFirebase Firestore
Threat events (type only: e.g. "unknown_install", "card_link_sms" + timestamp + app package name)Show admin dashboard statistics of neutralized threatsFirebase Firestore
App version, last-seen timestampActive-user counting; an uninstalled device drops out over timeFirebase Firestore

We do not collect: your name, phone number, SMS text content, notification content, contacts, photos, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not sell or share data with advertisers.

3. Permissions and why they are needed

PermissionWhy
READ_SMS, RECEIVE_SMSDetect card-linking / device-login phishing SMS on-device. SMS content stays on the device.
Notification access (NotificationListenerService)Optional. Reads only messaging-app notifications to detect RCS / anti-fraud ("Antifrod") messages that do not arrive as classic SMS (e.g. card-linking, device-login scams). Notification content is analyzed on-device and is never stored or transmitted off the device.
QUERY_ALL_PACKAGESAudit installed apps to detect malware and sideloaded threats.
Accessibility (InstallBlockAccessibilityService)Optional. When the system install dialog opens for an unknown-source APK, the App can auto-cancel the dangerous install. It reads no other app content and sends nothing to a server.
CAMERAScan QR codes.
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES/VIDEO/AUDIOCheck downloaded files for risky APKs.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS, VIBRATE, FOREGROUND_SERVICEReal-time threat alerts and background monitoring.
REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGESLet you remove a detected malicious app via the system dialog.

4. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication) — stores anonymous statistics and admin data. See Google's privacy policy.
  • VirusTotal (optional) — when checking a file, its SHA-256 hash (not the file itself) may be sent to VirusTotal to check against antivirus engines.

5. Data retention & deletion

Anonymous statistics are retained to show aggregate protection metrics. To request deletion of data associated with your anonymous device ID, contact us at the email above.

6. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.

BUXORO KIBER QALQON — Maxfiylik siyosati (O‘zbekcha)

BUXORO KIBER QALQON — qurilma ichida ishlaydigan kiberxavfsizlik vositasi. Biz maxfiyligingizni hurmat qilamiz.

1. Asosiy tamoyil — qurilmada tahlil

Xavfsizlik tahlili (SMS, ilovalar, fayllar, QR/URL) qurilmangizda bajariladi. SMS, xabarlar, suratlar, fayllar va kontaktlar mazmuni serverga yuborilmaydi.

2. Yig‘iladigan ma'lumotlar

Faqat anonim qurilma ID (tasodifiy SHA-256 hash — telefon raqami yoki IMEI EMAS), tahdid voqealari turi (matn emas) va ilova versiyasi. Ism, telefon raqami, SMS matni, joylashuv yoki shaxsiy ma'lumotlar yig‘ilmaydi. Ma'lumotlar sotilmaydi.

3. Ruxsatlar

READ_SMS/RECEIVE_SMS — qurilmada fishing SMS aniqlash; Bildirishnomalarga kirish (NotificationListener, ixtiyoriy) — RCS/Antifrod firibgarlik xabarlarini aniqlash (matn qurilmada tahlil qilinadi, serverga yuborilmaydi); QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — zararli ilovalarni aniqlash; Accessibility (ixtiyoriy) — noma'lum manbadan o'rnatishni avtomatik to'xtatish; CAMERA — QR kodlarni tekshirish.

4. Uchinchi tomon xizmatlari

Google Firebase (anonim statistika) va VirusTotal (fayl hash'i bo'yicha tekshiruv — faylning o'zi emas).

5. Ma'lumotni o'chirish

Anonim qurilma ID bilan bog'liq ma'lumotni o'chirish uchun yuqoridagi elektron pochtaga murojaat qiling.
